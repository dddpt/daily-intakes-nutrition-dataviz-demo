Personal Nutrition Data-viz Demo

Welcome on this personal nutrition datavizualisation! It is a demo built for a personal nutrition project where participants collect data on their food consumption. The proof-of-concept interactive visualization allows participants to compare their nutrient intakes across food classes with the rest of the population.
Have fun! ;-)
